Amazon Book Scraper

Paste an Amazon Best Sellers URL to download a full CSV of book data.

How to get the URL

1
Go to Amazon Best Sellers
2
Browse to any category you want (e.g. Paranormal Romance, Fantasy, etc.)
3
Copy the URL from your browser and paste it above
4
Click the button — your CSV will download automatically when done (takes around 5 mins for 100 books)
